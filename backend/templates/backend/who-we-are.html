How Wahine Was Born

We are women, friends, combining our knowledge and experience in capital markets and human rights empowerment work to bring about a better future for women and our community. We have come together to make women’s journey through life, more secure one step at a time.


Wahine Capital was set up during the COVID lockdown with the vision to empower women by securing our relationship with finance through knowledge and collaborative platforms. Wahine Capital hopes to be part of the change towards a more balanced and sustainable contribution of women in the economy and the society.

Our Purpose

Wahine Capital envisions a future where women have access to acquire the confidence, capability and knowledge to secure a sound financial future for themselves and their community. Women are now a Major Driving Force in the Economy. Whilst women’s consumerism has increased, our participation and ownership in the economy is not on par with men. Wahine Capital hopes to be part of the change towards a more balanced and sustainable contribution of women in the economy and the society.
