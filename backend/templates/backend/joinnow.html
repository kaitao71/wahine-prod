How We Protect You


Some of your most personal information is stored with us, which is why we've adopted the following best practices to keep you and your data safe.

SSL Encryption

SSL, or Secure Sockets Layer, is a protocol used for establishing secure connections between web servers and clients. It is typically used to protect sensitive information, such as credit card numbers and passwords, from being intercepted by attackers. W Vault uses SSL to prevent attackers from being able read or modify the data being transmitted on the platform.

Penetration Testing

W Vault will undergo penetration testing, also known as "pen testing" to help identify vulnerabilities in the system that an attacker could exploit, and provide an opportunity to fix these vulnerabilities before they are discovered and exploited by an actual attacker. By conducting regular pen tests, we can ensure that their web applications are as secure as possible and protect against potential attacks.
