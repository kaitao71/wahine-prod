SSL Encryption
SSL, or Secure Sockets Layer, is a protocol used for establishing secure connections between web servers and clients. It is typically used to protect sensitive information, such as credit card numbers and passwords, from being intercepted by attackers. W.Vault uses SSL to prevent attackers from being able read or modify the data being transmitted on the platform.
Penetration Testing
V Wault utilizes penetration testing, also known as "pen testing" to help identify vulnerabilities in the system that an attacker could exploit, and provide an opportunity to fix these vulnerabilities before they are discovered and exploited by an actual attacker.
