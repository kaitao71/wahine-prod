What is a Digital Vault?



Digital vaults are simply a digital safebox. It is a great way to secure and manage your digital assets and digital copies of your important stuff in an increasingly digital world. They provide a secure and centralized location for storing documents, passwords, and other sensitive information, protecting against data breaches and compliance violations. Our digital vault solution, W Vault is designed to simplify your life. In addition to storing important information, there is also a dashboard that will allow you to see, track and assess your important information and notify the necessary people at the right time.




What is the difference between notifier and access list?



This is an added value feature of W Vault where we have differentiated between the two. Who can be a notifier? A notifier is anyone you have identified as the person who will get in touch with us should a trigger event happen such as death, disability, mental incapacity and so on. She or he could be your lawyer, a family member, your best friend or any other trusted person. When we have verified the notifier and the trigger event, we will then provide access of all the information you have stored on W Vault to the people on your access list. Your access list individuals will most likely be your loved ones.
