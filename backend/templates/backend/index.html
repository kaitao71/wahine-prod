Our Purpose

Wahine Capital envisions a future where women have access to acquire the confidence, capability and knowledge to secure a sound financial future for themselves and their community. Women are now a Major Driving Force in the Economy But whilst women’s consumerism has increased, our participation and ownership in the economy is not on par with men.


The labour workforce presents a multitude of challenges for women - unequal opportunities, the gender pay gap, tendency towards the informal labour sector, safety at the workplace and many more.

How Wahine Was Born

We are three friends, three women, combining our knowledge and experience in capital markets and NGO work to bring about a better future for women and our community. We have come together to make women’s journey through life, more secure one step at a time.


Wahine Capital was set up during the COVID lockdown with the vision to empower women by securing our relationship with finance through knowledge and collaborative platforms. We hope to contribute to a future where women have easy access to acquire the confidence, capability and knowledge to secure a sound financial future for themselves and the society.
